12-Month Warranty

We strive to ensure that you have a stress-free ownership experience when you buy a car from CARS24. To achieve this, we refurbish each vehicle after conducting a thorough 140-point inspection to ensure they are ready for the road. In addition to this, we provide a 12-month warranty on each car we sell so you don't need to worry about any unforeseen maintenance or repair costs.

While we're hoping you never need to use it, here are details of the warranty.

frequently ask question

Is the warranty chargeable?

+ -

Each car we sell comes with a free warranty, valid for up to 12 months or 15,000 km, whichever comes first.

What’s not covered under warranty?

+ -

We have got the car covered for 12 months for the most crucial parts-engine, and the parts that connect power to the wheels. Anything else that is not covered can be found in the warranty booklet. Please note no consumables, wear & tear or accidental damages are covered under warranty.

What parts are used to repair the car under warranty?

+ -

Parts used for repair/replacement work are either original equipment supplier (OES) or original equipment manufacturer (OEM) depending upon the part’s availability and procurement time. Discretion to use OES or OEM lies with the warranty provider. Needless to say, OES parts used are at par with their OEM counterparts and deliver the same level of performance.

How can I claim the warranty?

+ -

Open the “My Bookings” Section on your website or app. Once you buy the car you want, you’ll be able to see a warranty section. Under “Warranty”, click on “Request Repair” to initiate the claim process. Our technical support team will contact you to understand the issue better and assist you further.Open the “My Car” section on the CARS24 application, click on “Purchased Cars” under “My Car” tab, select “Request warranty claim” to initiate the claim process. If there are any problems on your end, our technical support team will get in touch with you soon.

Can I get the warranty extended?

+ -

Our thorough refurbishment process and 12 month warranty ensures you have a trouble-free drive. At the moment we do not provide extended warranty.
